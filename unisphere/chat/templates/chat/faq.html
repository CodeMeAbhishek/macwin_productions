Frequently Asked Questions

What is Unisphere?

Unisphere is a social platform designed specifically for NU.ac.in students to connect, share, and communicate.

How do I create an account?

You can create an account using your NU.ac.in email address. Simply click on the "Sign Up" button and follow the registration process.

Is Unisphere free to use?

Yes, Unisphere is completely free for all NU.ac.in students.

How do I report inappropriate content?

You can report any inappropriate content by clicking the report button on the post or by contacting our support team.
